This three-part poster series reveals three fundamental concepts of design that leaders of the Bauhaus promoted. Grid Logic encourages the integration of fundamental forms in design, New Unity embraces universal forms and characteristics to develop a new design language, and Tactility urges young designers to explore the physicality of their work in order to grow and develop. By photographing my own hands and adhering to a strict grid system, each poster can relate to one another while still living on their own.
