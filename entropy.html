This three-part poster series explores the law of entropy through the manipulation of materiality, transparency, and reflection. The words used in these works are from Klaus Nürnberger’s book, Martin Luther’s Message for Us Today. The full text reads, “there is no construction without deconstruction, no life without the death of other lives, no order without chaos.” By segmenting the text into three parts, varying treatments were implemented from the physicality of the material itself to the weather conditions reflected by the mirror that each piece was photographed on.

Project Details

Completed for a poster class at Parsons School of Design. Words inspired by Klaus Nurburger's Martin Luther's Message To Us All. All photography, art direction and design by Whitney Badge.
