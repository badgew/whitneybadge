This concert poster series was inspired by the dream pop band, Little Dragon. The song Pretty Girls from their 2014 album uses a melancholic tone to set the scene. The song is about Los Angeles girls, the value placed in vanity, and is meant to celebrate beauty and power within. Inspired by nostalgic photography of women from the 60s, I created a concert poster series that uses the song's lyrics to create a narrative. Using a soft color scheme, an expressive serif, and circular elements to demote value placed in outward appearance, I further promote the song's strong messaging.

Project Details

Completed for a poster class at Parsons School of Design. All imagery was labeled with Creative Commons when sourced. The typeface used in this project is Bagnard.
