In celebration of its 5th birthday, Summer Fridays is hosting a weekend-long pop up in New York this March, 2023. They’re collaborating with Upside Pizza to takeover their space, blending New York City charm with the Summer Fridays feel. To dedicate this milestone moment for the brand, we created a pop-up identity for the event, complete with takeaway pizza boxes and branded merchandise.
