Youthful Pleasures
Short Film Titles + Collateral

Youthful Pleasures is an ensemble comedy about a dissatisfied 20-something coming of age 10 years too late.

For this film, we wanted the title treatment to walk the line between teenage nostalgia and the sobering reality of growing up. Images of the cast memebers were Xeroxed and scanned and placed in a row, much like a yearbook. The type is tight and whimsical, playing with letterforms to incorporate a sense of discovery. The hand-done doodles on the poster are tied into the title treatment by using the same marker to trace the title and turn into a stop-motion clip. With all this considered, our outcome was lively, earnest, and tactile.

Written and Directed by Rex Provost
