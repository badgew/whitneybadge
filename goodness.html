Goodness
Identity

Goodness is one of the only black-owned businesses in London that strives to make plant-based alternatives accessible and inspiring. We began by crafting the goodness mindset, asking what it means to bring goodness into every aspect of life, and how it can be used to build a sense of community. We then developed our visual approach, using bright colors, a fluid monogram, and floral symbolism to connect the products to its community.
