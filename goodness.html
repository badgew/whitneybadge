Goodness
Identity

Goodness is one of the only black-owned businesses in London, UK that strives to make plant-based alternatives accessible and inspiring. The founder, Doyin, approached me to come alongside her as a design partner to build everything from the ground up. Starting with the brand system, we focused on crafting the goodness mindset, asking what it means to bring goodness into every aspect of life, and how it can be used to build a sense of community.

Fluid and inviting, the goodness asterisk connects the caveat of plant-based m*lks with their goals for community-building. Beyond an asterisk, this symbol is used to connect the system together using a single, curvy, thread. The typeface in use is Beatrice Regular, as simple as it is expansive, which compliments the wordmark that combines traits of Beatrice with Mateusz Machalski’s ‘Afronaut’.
