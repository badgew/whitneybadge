Whitney Badge is a designer based in New York City. She holds a BFA in Communication Design from Parsons School of Design. As a strategic designer, she is compelled by the process of meaning-making as it relates to social anthropology and identity design.

She was recently selected as 1 of 2 individuals to participate in World's Greatest Internship, a 6-month program, traveling to 6 cities around the world and working for 6 agencies one month at a time. Currently, she is in New York City at Collins.
