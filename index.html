Whitney Badge is a graphic designer based in NYC. Her work is influenced by the relationship between meaning and value, which translates to identity and experience design across mediums both digital and physical.

She holds a BFA in Communication Design from Parsons, and recently participated in WGI: a 6-month experience working at 6 different agencies one month at a time. She is currently working at the Museum of Modern Art.
