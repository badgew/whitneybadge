Think, Talk, Make
Workshop Guide

On December 7th, 2018, Made Thought held their first ever 'To Think' workshop on 'Impact and Creative Opportunity'. Seeking to explore and understand how they can together create a thinking culture in which we evolve with the world, it brought together a small group of peoplo to talk over lunch.

This is a record of our first discussion, and a guidebook of best practice for workshops at Made Thought in the future. The result is a 10-page program, 5 question cards, and 1 poster to summarize the workshop.
