This is a typographic campaign that addresses the gender pay gap which remains prevalent in the United States today. Using data from the American Association of University Women, this campaign uses data to promote hard-hitting truths about equity inequalities.

Project Details

Completed for an advanced typography project at Parsons School of Design. All images belong to Whitney Badge.
