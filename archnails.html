Arch Nails
Identity

Arch is a nail polish brand that studies the trajectory of self expression. As we get to know ourselves, we incorporate new layers into our identity. Fashion and beauty makes this process really accessible, making space for people to discover their own selves through a product.

The design system for Arch is referential of this exploration—altering the shape of the A to mimic the shapes we file our nails when we step into a salon. Heroic and subtle, this mark emphasises our inherent need to change, defining more of ourselves through decisions we make in the every day.
